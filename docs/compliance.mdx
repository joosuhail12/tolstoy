# Compliance

Tolstoy is designed to meet the highest standards of security, privacy, and compliance to support enterprise and regulated industry requirements.

## Certifications and Standards

### SOC 2 Type II

**Status:** Certified  
**Scope:** Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality

Tolstoy maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance with annual audits by independent third-party auditors. Our controls cover:

- Information security policies and procedures
- System availability and performance monitoring
- Data processing integrity verification
- Confidentiality of customer information

**Report Access:** Available to enterprise customers under NDA

### ISO 27001

**Status:** Certified  
**Scope:** Information Security Management System

ISO 27001 certification demonstrates our commitment to:
- Systematic approach to managing sensitive information
- Risk-based security controls implementation
- Continuous monitoring and improvement
- Regular management reviews and audits

### GDPR Compliance

**Status:** Compliant  
**Scope:** Data protection and privacy rights

GDPR compliance features include:
- **Data Portability:** Export customer data in standard formats
- **Right to Deletion:** Automated data deletion workflows
- **Privacy by Design:** Built-in privacy protections
- **Data Processing Agreements:** Available for all customers
- **Data Minimization:** Collect only necessary data
- **Consent Management:** Granular consent controls

### HIPAA Compliance

**Status:** Available for BAA customers  
**Scope:** Healthcare information protection

For healthcare organizations, Tolstoy provides:
- **Business Associate Agreements (BAAs):** Legal compliance framework
- **End-to-End Encryption:** PHI protection in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access with audit logging
- **Data Retention:** Configurable retention policies
- **Incident Response:** Healthcare-specific breach procedures

### CCPA Compliance

**Status:** Compliant  
**Scope:** California Consumer Privacy Act

CCPA compliance includes:
- Consumer rights to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## Data Protection

### Data Classification

Tolstoy supports multiple data classification levels:

| Classification | Description | Controls |
|---------------|-------------|----------|
| Public | Non-sensitive information | Standard encryption |
| Internal | Business information | Enhanced access controls |
| Confidential | Sensitive business data | Strict access controls, logging |
| Restricted | Highly sensitive/regulated | Maximum security, approval workflows |
